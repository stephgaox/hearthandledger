# Design Context — Family Budget Dashboard

## Audience
- Primary: Personal family use (2–5 members), initially private
- Future: Public deployment for other families
- Context: Used frequently throughout the month, not just monthly reviews

## Use Cases
- Track spending vs income in real time
- Upload bank/credit card statements
- Categorize and review transactions
- Spot trends across months and accounts

## Brand Personality
- **Tone**: Friendly and warm, yet information-rich and trustworthy
- **Feel**: Like a personal tool you enjoy opening — not cold or bank-sterile, not flashy
- **Reference**: Notion (clean, structured, calm) with dynamic interactive features
- **One word**: Trustworthy

## Aesthetic Direction
- Light mode primary (clean, readable, daylight use)
- Parchment/kraft paper palette: background `#f5f1e6`, card `#fffcf5`, border `#dbd0ba`
- Primary accent: warm sienna brown `#a67c52` — trustworthy, earthy, not corporate
- Income: muted forest green `#4a7c59` | Expense: terracotta `#b54a35` | Savings: warm olive `#6b7a3e`
- Typography: Libre Baskerville (UI), Lora (serif/display), IBM Plex Mono (numbers/mono)
- Motion: Subtle and purposeful — data loads smoothly, transitions are calm
- No glassmorphism, no gradient text, no hero metric layout

## Anti-Goals
- NOT a sterile banking app
- NOT flashy or gamified
- NOT overwhelming with color
- NOT generic AI aesthetic (cyan-on-dark, purple gradients, glow effects)
